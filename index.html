About Me

Steve Babb is a senior executive, strategic advisor, and enterprising leader with a distinguished record in creating a market-informed focus on product and service, brand, and overall corporate growth strategy. He is a trusted advisor to boards of directors and executive leaders, confidently bridging the gap between B2B/B2C customers and decision makers. His work has influenced major innovation launches and brand concepts for companies like Microsoft, Kaiser-Permanente, HP, Sprint, Johnson & Johnson, Verizon, State Farm, Craig Hospital, LucasArts, Zulily, PayPal, Interface, and The Pokémon Company.

As the President of Verus Group, Steve has been the architect and leader of more than 5,000 customer-facing market tests for hundreds of product, service, and marketing concepts (organic development & M&A). He has built a team of researchers and strategic partners whose work spans North America, Europe, Asia, and Latin America. Steve founded the group in 1998 and has propelled the success of the business through long-standing relationships of trust.

Earlier in his career, Steve served as a Brand Director and Associate Director of Market Research for US West. In this role, led the execution team for the Salt Lake City Olympics brand partnership and developed brand assets to support the Denver Broncos sponsorship.

The trajectory of Steve’s career began at DDB where, as a Strategic Planner, he provided market insights that influenced the growth strategy for brands like Rubbermaid, Anheuser-Busch, The Dial Corporation, Discover, Volkswagen, and Michelob. Steve holds M.S. and B.S. degrees in economics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He has been invited to speak as a guest lecturer at the University of Kansas on the topic of “Net Neutrality and the Opposing Interests of Network Operators and Consumers.”
